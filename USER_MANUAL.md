## Introduction

The AI-Driven Crop Disease Prediction and Management System helps farmers quickly identify crop diseases using their smartphone camera and provides actionable treatment recommendations.

### Key Features

- **Instant Disease Detection**: Take a photo of your crop and get results in seconds
- **High Accuracy**: ML models trained on thousands of crop images for reliable detection
- **Treatment Guidance**: Step-by-step instructions for treating identified diseases
- **Offline Capable**: Works without internet connectivity
- **Multiple Crops**: Supports various crop types including rice, wheat, tomato, potato, and more
- **History Tracking**: Save and review past diagnoses

## Disease Detection

### Taking a Photo

1. Tap the camera button on the home screen
2. Point your camera at the affected plant part
3. Ensure good lighting (natural light preferred)
4. Get close enough to see details clearly
5. Tap the capture button

### Uploading a Photo

1. Tap the camera button
2. Select "Upload from Gallery"
3. Choose a clear, well-lit photo
4. Tap "Analyze"

### Tips for Best Results

- Use natural lighting when possible
- Focus on the affected area (leaves, stems, fruits)
- Avoid shadows and glare
- Take multiple angles if needed
- Ensure the image is in focus

## Understanding Results

### Prediction Display

After analysis, you'll see:

#### Disease Name
The identified or suspected disease (e.g., "Tomato Early Blight")

#### Confidence Score
A percentage indicating how confident the model is in the prediction:
- **High (80-100%)**: Very likely correct
- **Medium (50-79%)**: Possible match, consider consulting an expert
- **Low (< 50%)**: Uncertain result, try retaking the photo

#### Severity Assessment
- **Mild**: Early stage, easy to treat
- **Moderate**: Active infection, treatment recommended soon
- **Severe**: Advanced infection, immediate action needed

#### Confidence Indicators
The app shows up to 3 possible diseases with their confidence scores, allowing you to consider alternative diagnoses.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### App Crashes on Launch
- Restart your device
- Update to the latest app version
- Reinstall if issue persists

#### Camera Not Working
- Check camera permissions in device settings
- Ensure no other app is using the camera
- Restart the app

#### Analysis Takes Long Time
- Ensure stable internet connection (for cloud processing)
- Try smaller/lower resolution photos
- Check available storage space

#### Incorrect Prediction
- Retake the photo with better lighting
- Try a different angle
- Focus on the most affected area
- Consult a local agricultural expert if uncertain

## FAQ

**Q: How accurate is the disease detection?**
A: Our models achieve over 90% accuracy on test datasets. However, results should be verified with local experts for critical decisions.

**Q: Does the app work without internet?**
A: Yes, you can capture photos offline. Analysis will complete when you reconnect. Some content may be limited.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. We prioritize your privacy. Images are processed securely and not shared without consent.

**Q: What crops are supported?**
A: Currently supports: Rice, Wheat, Tomato, Potato, Cotton, Maize, and more. We're constantly adding new crops.

**Q: How often is the disease database updated?**
A: The database is updated quarterly with the latest agricultural research.

**Q: Can I use this for organic farming?**
A: Yes! The app provides both organic and chemical treatment options.

**Q: What should I do if the prediction seems wrong?**
A: Retake the photo in better conditions, or consult with a local agricultural expert for confirmation.
